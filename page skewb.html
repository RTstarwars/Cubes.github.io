The Skewb


1. The first thing that you have to do is play around with the cube and figure out how it works. It can be a bit confusing at first, but once you are comfortable with moving it, then it will be easier.
2. Then choose colour and solve just that one side.
3. To solve one side, find the middle of that colour then look for other pieces of that colour.
